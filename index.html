Our Vision

We beleive in the power of music.
Music can shape our communities through both inciting mutual inspiration
and provoking sprited intellectual critique.
However, we have noticed that while there exist platforms for facilitating
this essential culterial discourse around other forms of media such as movies, books, and videogames.
There does not yet exist a platform which fully lends itself to the unique nature of musical culteral discourse.
Enter Us.
