Our Vision

We beleive in the power of music.
Music can shape our communities through both inciting mutual inspiration
and provoking sprited intellectual critique.
However, we have noticed that while there exist platforms for facilitating
this essential culterial discourse around other forms of media such as movies, books, and videogames.
There does not yet exist a platform which fully lends itself to the unique nature of musical culteral discourse.
Enter MyTunes.

Features

MyTunes is built from the ground up to facilitate thoughtfull discourse in the world of music.
This makes the inclusion of engaging features which specifically lend themselves to interacting with music essential.
Explore our list of exciting features below!

  • Upload, review, and rate your favorite songs on your MyTunes profile using our unique, innovative MyRate system
  • Explore your MyTunes feed to see what your friends are up to
  • Rate your friends' music taste using the MyRate system
  • Curate public playlists of songs for your MyTunes friends to enjoy
  • Comment on a specific moment in a song you or another user has uploaded and it will appear for other listeners at the correct moment in real time as the song is playing
  • View the average rating of any song by geographic region, age group, or other demographic filters
  • Don't like a take? You can both upvote and downvote fellow MyTunes users' reviews, and even provide a counter-rating along with your own take in the comments
  • Find new friends using our MyTunes Matches algorithm which pairs you based on your musical tastes!
